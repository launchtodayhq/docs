## What is Superwall?

Superwall is a paywall optimization platform that helps you maximize subscription revenue through:

- **A/B Testing** - Test different paywall designs and copy
- **Remote Configuration** - Update paywalls without app updates
- **Analytics** - Track conversion rates and revenue impact
- **Targeting** - Show different paywalls to different user segments

## When to Use

Consider Superwall if you:

- Want to optimize paywall conversion rates
- Need to A/B test pricing and messaging
- Want to update paywalls without app releases
- Have significant user volume to test with
