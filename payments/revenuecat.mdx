## What is RevenueCat?

RevenueCat is a subscription management platform that simplifies in-app purchases across iOS, Android, and web. It handles:

- **Receipt validation** - Verify purchases with Apple and Google
- **Cross-platform sync** - Share subscription status across devices
- **Analytics** - Track revenue, churn, and subscriber metrics
- **Entitlements** - Manage feature access based on subscription status

Consider RevenueCat if you:

- Build for both iOS and Android
- Want simplified subscription management
- Need cross-device subscription sync
- Want detailed revenue analytics
